Meet the Founder!

Alpomish, the founder of CatsAndFriends Uz, has always believed in the magical bond between humans and cats. Inspired by his own journey of rescuing and caring for stray cats, Alpomish envisioned a space where people could experience the joy of spending time with these amazing creatures. By creating the CatCafe, he aimed to build a community that not only shares a love for cats but actively contributes to their well-being.
