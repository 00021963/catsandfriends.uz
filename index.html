📋RULES
For a comfortable and safe time in our cafe, please read our internal rules for visiting. By visiting the Cats And Friends cat cafe, you agree to our terms and accept the rules specified below.
These rules establish the procedure for guests to stay in the Cats And Friends cat cafe (hereinafter referred to as the cat cafe).
- When entering the cat cafe, you must put on shoe covers, they are available at the entrance (Make sure that the shoe covers are put on correctly and are not torn, you can change the shoe covers at any time).
The restaurant premises are under video surveillance
- When entering and leaving, treat your hands with an antiseptic.
- Refuse to visit the cat cafe if you are...allergic to cats. The administration of the cat cafe is not responsible for the manifestation of an allergic reaction.
- Children under 12 years old fully accompanied by parents (adults) you are fully responsible for your child and their actions.
- Do not leave children unattended, hold small children by the hand.
- When communicating with cats, remember their desires and needs. If you come with a child, show the child by your example how to communicate with a cat. Any animal, even the most affectionate and fluffy, has claws and teeth.
- Touch the cats carefully. Move calmly, without sudden movements.
- Do not touch, pet, or tease the cats that the cat sitters have highlighted in "red".
- Do not do anything against the will of the cats! Cats cannot be woken up, held by force in your arms, pulled by the paws / ears / tails, lifted to the second tier, fed without the administrator's permission, etc.
